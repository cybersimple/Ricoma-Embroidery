EM SERIES

The EM Series is a 10-needle home embroidery machine and is perfect for embroidery on caps, flats, finished garments and more!

Features include:
- Ribbon LED lighting
- Thread break detection
- Automatic color change
- Adjustable control panel
- 7” user-friendly touch screen panel
- 220° wide angle cap system

Features include:
- Thread break detection
